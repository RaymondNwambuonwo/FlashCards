Flash Cards

Welcome to Music Flash! This is a simple game that is something of a hybrid between trivia and flash cards. In order to play, simply input your answer in the box below and click submit to see if you are right. If you are correct you can proceed to the next and recieve a point. If you are wrong you will be prompted to try again and in addition to that, loose a point. Also if you are wrong, you can click the picture to reveal the answer to type in. Enjoy!
